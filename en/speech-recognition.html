TECHNICAL FEATURES

Supported languages

  • Russian (Cloud/On-Premise)
  • English (Cloud/On-Premise)
  • Kazakh (Cloud/On-Premise)
  • Uzbek (Cloud/On-Premise)

Audio data formats

Telephony (phone):

Wav PCM, 8 kHz /16 bit

Media (broadcast):

Wav PCM, 16 kHz /16 bit

Audio requirements

All major formats and codecs WAV, AAC, OGG, MP3, WMA, MuLaw, ALaw, Linear16, RawOpus are supported and automatically converted into WAV.
