TECHNICAL FEATURES

Supported voices and languages

  • Russian: male, female
  • English: male, female
  • Kazakh: male, female
  • Uzbek: male, female

On-premise minimum hardware requirements

  • 2 CPUs (2 physical cores) with a frequency of 2.4 GHz
  • NVIDIA GPU with CUDA and at least 8 GB RAM
  • 8 GB RAM
  • 30 GB of available disk space

Technical requirements

  • 64-bit Linux-based operating system (CentOS version not lower than 7.X, Debian version not lower than 10.x, Astra Linux version not lower than 1.7)
  • docker, nvidia-docker, docker-compose
  • CUDA driver version 10.2+

Output data format

  • Sampling frequency: 22050 Hz
  • Codec: pcm_s16le
  • Number of channels: 1
