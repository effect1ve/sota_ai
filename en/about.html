ABOUT THE COMPANY

Sota AI is an Uzbek company that offers state-of-the-art solutions and world-class products in the field of speech technologies, voice biometrics, as well as specialized services for intelligent processing of unstructured data, and other solutions based on artificial intelligence.

Sota AI professionals have unique technological competencies in the field of developing and building complex information-analytical systems and platform solutions for analyzing text and audio information using proprietary technologies based on artificial intelligence, machine learning and natural language processing/understanding systems (AI/ML/NLU).

Our services and technologies are designed for major banks, insurance companies, telecom companies, real estate developers, online retailers, airlines, hotlines, and many other technology-oriented companies.
